User Support

How do I create a SmartRide account?

Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email to start using SmartRide.

Can I book a ticket without logging in?

No, logging in is required to ensure a secure and personalized experience including access to your travel history and e-tickets.

Troubleshooting

I can’t track my bus. What should I do?

Ensure your internet connection is active. If the issue persists, it might be a temporary server delay. Try refreshing the page or contact support.

Payment failed, but money was deducted. What now?

Please wait 15-30 minutes. If the transaction was unsuccessful, your amount will be refunded automatically. You can also report the issue via the contact form.

Common Queries

How can I cancel my ticket?

Go to the 'My Bookings' section after logging in and click on 'Cancel Ticket'. Refunds, if applicable, will be processed as per policy.

Is SmartRide available in my city?

We are rapidly expanding. Visit the 'Routes' page to see if SmartRide operates in your area. If not, stay tuned for updates!

Can I use SmartRide on my mobile?

Yes! SmartRide is fully responsive and accessible from your smartphone's browser. A dedicated app is also coming soon.
